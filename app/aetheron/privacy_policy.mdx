# Privacy Policy for Aetheron

**Last Updated: June 13, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Aetheron. I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how I handle information during your use of my application.

## About Aetheron

Aetheron is a pass-and-play game designed for local multiplayer gameplay on a single device. My application operates completely offline and does not collect any user data.

## Information Collection

### No Data Collection

I do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information from your device. Specifically:

-   No app analytics are collected or stored
-   No game usage statistics are tracked
-   No user behavior data is monitored
-   No crash reports are sent to me

All game data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

### Device Permissions

My app does not request any special device permissions beyond basic app functionality.

### Information You Provide

-   **Game State Data**: I store your current game progress locally on your device so you can resume games after closing and reopening the app. This information never leaves your device.

## Data Storage and Security

-   All game-related data is stored locally on your device
-   No data is transmitted to external servers
-   No analytics or tracking tools are implemented in the app

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Children's Privacy

My application is suitable for users of all ages. I do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Future Updates

If I add new features that affect data usage or privacy in the future, I will update this privacy policy before releasing such features.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
